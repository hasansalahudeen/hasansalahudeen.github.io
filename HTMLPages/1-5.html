Almond Green Salad

Fresh and Crispy

Ingredients

1/2 pound bacon
2 tablespoons white wine vineger
3 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon dry hot mustard
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2teaspoon ground peperika
1/4cup olive oil
1 head red leaf lettuce torn into bite size pieces
1 (15 ounce) can of mandarin oranges, drained
1 bunch green onios, diced
3/4 cup silvered almonds

Directions

1. In medium skillet over medium-heat, cook bacon until evenly brown. Drain cool, and crumble
2. To make the dressing, thoroughly blend the vineger, honey, dry mustard, celery salt, paprika, and olive oil.
3. Place lettuce, oranges, green onions, bacon, and almonds in serving bowl. Toss with dressing and serve.
